Our new paper is available on [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/)!

***
Our paper entitled [FormalRewardBench: A Benchmark for Formal Theorem Proving Reward Models]({{ '/assets/pdf/2605.10141v1.pdf' | relative_url }}) is available on [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.10141). We introduce the first benchmark for evaluating reward models in formal theorem proving with Lean 4, consisting of 250 preference pairs generated through five expert-curated error injection strategies.
