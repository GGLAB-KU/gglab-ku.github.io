Overview of the SIGTURK 2026 Shared Task: Terminology-Aware Machine Translation for English-Turkish Scientific Texts, presented at EACL 2026.

***
We presented the findings and overview of the SIGTURK 2026 Shared Task at the [SIGTURK Workshop during EACL 2026](https://sigturk.github.io/). The shared task challenged teams on three subtasks: detecting and aligning technical terms, correcting term translations using expert-provided hints, and full end-to-end post-editing with an offline terminology glossary. A fine-tuned 4B model beat our best baseline (GPT-5.2) on end-to-end post-editing, but models still fall well behind human experts when it comes to following expert terminology.
